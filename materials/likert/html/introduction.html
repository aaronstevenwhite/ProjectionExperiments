In this experiment, you will be given pairs of verbs along with a 7-point similarity scale. Your job will be to rate the similarity between the meanings of the two verbs from very dissimilar (1) to very similar (7).

For instance, if you received the verbs eat and devour, you might respond with a high similarity rating—perhaps a 6 or a 7—since the meanings of eat and devour are quite similar. On the other hand, if you received the verbs kill and kiss, you might respond with a low similarity rating—perhaps a 1 or a 2—since the meanings of kill and kiss are quite dissimilar.

The experiment will begin with a practice to get you used to the task. After this, a message will tell you that you are moving on to the experiment.
